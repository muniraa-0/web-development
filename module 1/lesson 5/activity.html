Facts about pet shop

A pet shop, also known as a pet store, is a retail establishment that sells pets, pet food, supplies, and accessories for domestic animals. Products and services Live animals: Common pets include fish, birds, small mammals like hamsters and rabbits, and reptiles. Pet supplies: This category includes a wide range of products such as pet food, toys, beds, leashes, collars, grooming tools, and cages. Accessories: Pet shops often sell decorative items for pet habitats, such as aquariums or terrariums.
